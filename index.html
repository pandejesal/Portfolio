WSB Alpha — Quantitative Sentiment & NLP Engine

An end-to-end Python research pipeline (`wsb_alpha_system.py`) isolating financial alpha from Reddit "Due Diligence" posts using FinBERT and Oxford growth normalization.

Data Ingestion Apify Puppeteer Scraper (r/WallStreetBets "DD" Flair)
NLP Sentiment FinBERT Transformer (Financial Domain Classification)
Growth Normalization Oxford Subreddit Share Model (Mentions / Total Daily DDs)
Execution Alignment Strict T+1 Market Close Entry (Zero Look-Ahead Bias)

Core Pipeline Architecture & Methodology

  • Ticker Collision Resolution: Custom Regex + Financial Blacklist resolves uppercase word-ticker ambiguities (e.g. distinguishing "FOR", "BUY", "ON", "ATH" from real tickers).
  • FinBERT Confidence Filtering: Evaluates deep learning sentiment probabilities; discards low-confidence ambiguous posts to maintain high signal purity.
  • Oxford Subreddit Growth Model: Normalizes daily mention counts by total DD post volume, preventing platform expansion from artificially inflating sentiment metrics.
  • Look-Ahead Bias Elimination: Forces trade entry strictly at T+1 close (one full trading day post-publication) and evaluates forward 1-90 day excess returns against SPY.
  • Re-Evaluation & Database Engine: Merges new runs into `wsb_factual_research_data.csv`, dropping duplicate post-ticker entries and auto-populating elapsed returns via `yfinance`.
